Predicting Apnea Episodes

One potential future improvement to the detection and treatment of apnea could be the development of more advanced predictive models that can identify patients who are at high risk of developing apnea before symptoms occur. This would allow for earlier interventions, potentially preventing the onset of the condition altogether. This could be done using a regression equation with multiple variables and seeing how well an individual's vitals follows the curve generated.

Connecting To Your Smartwatch

Another potential improvement could be accessing the data directly from your smartwatch. This would provide a better user experience since individuals would not have to export their data and convert it each time. Instead, they could connect to their smartwatch account and automatically receive their information.
