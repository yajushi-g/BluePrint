Data Submission

Please submit your data to this page. You can export data from your smartwatch (such an Apple Watch, Fitbit, or Samsung Watch) and submit it to the form below. Our program only accepts .csv files so make sure to convert your file to the appropriate type. Please include as many of the following vitals as possible:

  • Heart Rate
  • Breathing Rate
  • Oxygen Saturation
  • Systolic Blood Pressure
  • Diastolic Blood Pressure

How The Data Is Analyzed

We will process your data by going through the .csv file and iterate through each time period. Then, it will look through each of the vitals that were provided. If there is a sudden change between the vitals over the time periods, we will alert you of a potential apnea episode.
